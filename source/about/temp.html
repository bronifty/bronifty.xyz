About me

I am a software engineer currently working in the Data industry with an interest at the intersection of modern pipelines with wasm, serverless and kubernetes (think Fluvio and Kubeflow) as well as React on the frontend for apps, even a little Angular for the patterns and some other things. I'm not well known as a creator of anything in particular, mostly a generalist who enjoys talking to people about various things. I'd like to see what's possible with putting everything in WASM modules on the edge as serverless functions and for the rest either orchestrate it with containers or do something else like Hashicorp's system for managing servers of all different types in their app production paradigm. Terraform seems like a pretty standard tool for managing infrastructure declaratively, but I'm also interested in other formats such as Argo gitops and Score.dev, which are abstractions to facilitate IaC. Ultimately, I'd like to work on fun technology and deploy like an SRE. The best way to reach me is on Twitter
